# Account Access
Customers can access their accounts via the mobile app, web portal, or phone support. For security, the bank never asks for full card numbers, PINs, or SMS codes.

## Password Reset
If a customer forgets the password, guide them to use the "Forgot password" flow. Verify identity using approved security questions and a one-time email link.

## Login Lockout
After five failed attempts, the account is locked for 30 minutes. Advise customers to wait or reset the password if urgent.

# Card Operations
Card delivery takes 3-5 business days. A digital card is available immediately after approval.

## Card Freeze
Customers can freeze a card in the app. Freezing is instant and reversible without fees.

## Card Replacement
If the card is lost or stolen, advise to freeze the card and request a replacement. The replacement fee is waived for the first incident per year.

# Transfers
Domestic transfers are processed within 1 business day. International transfers may take 2-4 business days.

## Transfer Limits
Standard daily limit is $5,000. Customers can request a temporary increase through verified support.

## Canceling Transfers
Transfers can be canceled within 15 minutes if still pending. After processing begins, cancellation is not guaranteed.

# Fees
The basic checking account has no monthly fee when the customer maintains a $500 balance.

## Overdraft
Overdraft protection is optional and must be enabled by the customer. Fees are $20 per incident when enabled.

## International Fees
International card usage incurs a 1.5% FX fee. ATM withdrawals abroad add a $3 flat fee.

# Disputes
Dispute claims should be opened within 60 days of the transaction. Provide the customer with a case ID and expected resolution time of 10 business days.

## Chargeback Status
Customers can track dispute status in the app under "Disputes." Notifications are sent by email.

# KYC & Compliance
The bank may request identity verification if suspicious activity is detected. Customers should be directed to the secure verification portal.

## Sensitive Data Policy
Never request full card numbers, PINs, or SMS codes in chat or phone. Collect only last 4 digits and document reference ID.

# Service Levels
Phone support is available 24/7. Chat support operates 8am-8pm local time.

## Escalation
Escalate to tier 2 when fraud is suspected or if the customer reports an account takeover.

# App Troubleshooting
If the app crashes, advise updating to the latest version and reinstalling if needed.

## Notifications
Customers can enable or disable notifications in Settings > Notifications.
